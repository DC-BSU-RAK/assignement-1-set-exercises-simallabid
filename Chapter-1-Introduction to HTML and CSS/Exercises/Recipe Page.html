Brownie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • Sugar: 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • Eggs: 2 large eggs
  • Vanilla extract: 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder: 1/3 cup (40g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Flour: 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • Salt: 1/4 tsp salt
  • Baking powder: 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • Chocolate chips: 1/2 cup (90g) chocolate chips or chunks (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 175Â°C (350Â°F). Grease and line an 8x8 inch (20x20 cm) baking pan.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan or microwave.
  3. Stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla into the melted butter.
  4. Add cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder. Mix until well combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips (if using).
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  7. Bake for 20â€“25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  8. Let it cool before cutting into squares. Enjoy!
