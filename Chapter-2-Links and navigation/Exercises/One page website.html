The Haunting Of Hill House

The Haunting of Hill House follows the Crain family, who move into a haunted mansion to renovate it. The mother, Olivia, dies mysteriously, and the family flees. Years later, the now-grown siblings—Steven, Shirley, Theo, Luke, and Nell—are still dealing with trauma from the house. When Nell returns to Hill House and dies, the family reunites and faces the dark secrets of their past. The story shifts between past and present, blending ghostly horror with themes of grief, trauma, and family.
